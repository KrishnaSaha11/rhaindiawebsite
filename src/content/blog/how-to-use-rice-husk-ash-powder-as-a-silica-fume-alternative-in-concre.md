Concrete producers are always trying to save money and cut emissions. Our rice husk ash powder helps you do both, acting as a smart, eco-friendly additive for your mix.

Why it matters:
✔ Acts as a cost-effective silica fume alternative.
✔ Fills tiny gaps to make your concrete more dense.
✔ Protects structures from harsh salts and acids.
✔ Drops your carbon footprint for green building.

How exactly does it help? According to a 2025 study in MDPI Sustainability, replacing up to 30% of your cement with this amorphous silica powder can cut CO2 emissions by about 40%. Because it is incredibly fine (much finer than standard cement), it acts as a strong supplementary cementitious material SCM (a powder that hardens alongside cement) to boost long-term durability.
