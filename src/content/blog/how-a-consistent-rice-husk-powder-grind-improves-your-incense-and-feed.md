🌸 Finding a Clean Burning Base for Incense Sticks

Making great incense sticks requires a fine base that binds well. 
When your raw powder is uneven, your final product suffers.

Many manufacturers struggle with powders that have too much moisture. Sometimes the particle size changes from bag to bag. This causes your dough to break during mixing. It can also make the final sticks burn poorly or go out.

You need a reliable base material that acts predictably. A good powder mixes fast and holds its shape.

Why it matters:
✔ Burns cleanly and very smoothly.
✔ Binds easily with natural gums.
✔ Holds expensive fragrances very well.
✔ Keeps stick shapes perfectly straight.
✔ Lowers your daily raw material costs.
