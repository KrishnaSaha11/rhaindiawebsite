🪵 Making Better Particle Boards with Rice Husk Powder

Making wood-polymer composites or particle boards? Finding good filler material can be tough, and wood dust is getting expensive.

We grind pure raw rice husk into a fine, organic powder. It is a brilliant, low-cost filler for board manufacturing.

Why it matters:

✔ Cuts down your wood and resin costs.
✔ Mixes smoothly into plastic and board composites.
✔ Gives uniform strength across every board.
✔ Saves trees by using renewable farm materials.

Our rice husk powder is packed with natural cellulose and lignin. This makes it a great organic binder for your factory. Just to be clear, this is pure ground raw husk, not ash. It provides a very consistent grind for smooth production lines.
