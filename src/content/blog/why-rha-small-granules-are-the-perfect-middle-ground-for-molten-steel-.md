🌋 Finding the Sweet Spot for Steel Insulation

Want to cover molten steel fast without fighting dust? Our rice husk ash small granules give you the best of both worlds.

As an experienced rice husk ash supplier India, we talk to steel plant managers every day. We know the daily struggle on the shop floor. Fine powders can blow around in plant drafts. This creates a messy workspace and wastes good material. Large granules keep the air clean, but they might spread a bit too slowly for some specific setups.

That is exactly why we size these small granules to 0.5-2 mm. You get a reliable molten steel insulation material that behaves perfectly. It drops right where you want it and forms a clean, protective crust almost instantly.

Why it matters:

✔ Spreads rapidly over liquid metal surfaces.
✔ Keeps the air clean for your workers.
✔ Prevents costly temperature drops in the melt.
✔ Delivers 90%+ amorphous high silica rice husk ash.

Keeping your liquid metal hot is critical for quality steel. A good ladle covering compound reduces radiation heat loss on liquid steel before continuous casting (source: IspatGuru). 

Our small granules work perfectly as a tundish insulation powder. They give you that ideal, even barrier. This blocks heat from escaping into the air. It also protects the steel melt from picking up unwanted oxygen.
