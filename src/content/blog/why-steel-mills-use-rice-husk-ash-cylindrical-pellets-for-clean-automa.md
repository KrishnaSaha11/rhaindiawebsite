🏭 Dust-Free Dosing: Rice Husk Ash Cylindrical Pellets for Steel Plants

Managing fine powder in a busy steel melting shop can be a messy job. That is why we press our high silica rice husk ash into neat cylindrical pellets.

Why it matters:
✔ Stops dust clouds during mechanical feeding.
✔ Uniform cylinders melt evenly over hot metal.
✔ Blocks heat from escaping the molten steel.
✔ Keeps plant floors cleaner and workers safer.

According to steel industry resource IspatGuru, a good ladle covering compound reduces radiation heat loss on liquid steel before continuous casting.
