🏭 Keep Your Steel Melting Shop Dust-Free with RHA Cylindrical Pellets

Pouring fine insulation over liquid steel often creates a messy dust cloud. It clogs machines and wastes good material to the plant draft.

Our Rice Husk Ash Cylindrical Pellets solve this problem completely. We compact our high SiO2 rice husk ash into neat, uniform cylinders for clean, easy application.

Why it matters:
✔ Densified shape means almost zero dust on the shop floor.
✔ Uniform cylinders run easily through mechanical and auto-feeders.
✔ Material melts evenly to create a strong, protective layer.
✔ Perfect for continuous casting unit insulation.

Why insulate the steel? According to industry sources like IspatGuru, a good ladle covering compound reduces radiation heat loss on liquid steel before continuous casting. Our pellets do exactly this. They give you the thermal benefits of a high-quality molten metal covering compound, but in a smart, easy-to-handle form.
