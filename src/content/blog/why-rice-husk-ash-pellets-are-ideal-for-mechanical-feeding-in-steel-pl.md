⚙️ Easier Mechanical Feeding for Steel Plants

Feeding insulation material into your furnace shouldn't create a massive cloud of dust. If your steel melting shop struggles with messy powder handling, changing the shape of your material helps.

Why it matters:
✔ Cylindrical shape means clean and easy machine feeding.
✔ Densified heavy pellets will not blow away under strong drafts.
✔ Melts and spreads evenly over molten steel to lock in heat.
✔ Keeps the air cleaner for your shop floor workers.

As a trusted ladle covering compound supplier, we know plant efficiency matters. According to IspatGuru, ladle covering compounds reduce radiation heat loss on liquid steel. This happens right before the metal enters the continuous caster.
