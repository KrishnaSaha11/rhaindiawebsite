🌋 Get Faster Heat Protection on Molten Steel

When you pour molten steel, every second of heat loss costs your plant money. You need to cover the surface quickly to trap that heat.

But pouring fine dust can create a huge mess in your factory air. Large pellets might take too long to melt and spread. That is exactly why we make Rice Husk Ash Small Granules.

These 0.5-2mm granules hit the sweet spot for your steel melting shop. They are small enough to spread instantly. Yet, they are heavy enough to stay right where you put them.

Why it matters:

✔ Spreads fast across liquid metal
✔ Keeps your plant air cleaner
✔ Traps heat inside the ladle
✔ Melts smoothly without large clumps

According to steel industry resources like IspatGuru, a quality ladle covering compound reduces radiation heat loss before continuous casting. Our small granules do exactly that.

Because they are made from combusted ash, they pack over 90% amorphous silica. This makes them a highly effective metallurgical insulation powder. They create a strong thermal barrier to protect your liquid metal.
