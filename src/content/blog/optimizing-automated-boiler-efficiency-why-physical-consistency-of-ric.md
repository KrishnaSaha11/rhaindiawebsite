🔄 Eliminate Boiler Feed Clogs: Anti-Clogging Rice Husk Pellets for Automated Combustion Systems

For industrial manufacturing plants operating automated stoker or grate boilers, fuel consistency is critical. Uneven fuel sizing, excessive moisture, and loose biomass dust lead to feed-hopper bridging, fuel-line blockages, and irregular thermal output.

At Ambika Rice Mill & Ambika Biotech (RHA India), we address these operational pain points with our high-density, precisely sized Rice Husk Pellets. Engineered from raw, organic agricultural waste in Sambalpur, Odisha, these pellets offer a highly structured, renewable alternative to raw biomass, preventing mechanical downtime while lowering your carbon footprint.

Why global boiler operators and energy plants in Germany, Poland, South Korea, and Japan trust our raw biomass pellets:

✔ Predictable Thermal Output: Delivers a consistent calorific value of 3,200–3,800 kcal/kg, ensuring stable combustion temperatures without sudden drops.

✔ Seamless Automated Feeding: Uniform cylindrical pellet sizing eliminates bridging and clogging in automatic screw feeders and gravity-fed stoker systems.

✔ Low Moisture & High Density: Our mechanical compaction process drives out excess moisture, increasing combustion efficiency and reducing flue gas volume.

✔ Sustainable Coal Substitution: Ideal for co-firing applications, allowing heavy industries to meet strict emission reduction targets without costly boiler retrofits.

✔ Low Residue Profile: Unlike low-grade biomass fuels, our raw husk pellets produce a predictable, manageable ash volume, simplifying grate cleaning.
